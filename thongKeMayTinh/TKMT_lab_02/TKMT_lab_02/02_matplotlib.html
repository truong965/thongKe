VẼ ĐỒ THỊ CON¶

Bn c th v nhiu th con trong cng mt th ln. Cc th con c t chc dng ma trn bng cc khai bo s dng, s ct trong hm subplot(). Tham s th 3 trong hm subplot() th hin ch mc ca th c nh s t 1 theo th t t tri sang phi, t trn xung di.

Chi tit c th tham kho y (LINK)

In [15]:
x = np.linspace(-1.4, 1.4, 30)
plt.subplot(2, 2, 1)
plt.plot(x, x)         # ma trn  th con 2 dng, 2 ct,  th th nht tri trn
  
plt.subplot(2, 2, 2)

plt.plot(x, x**3)

plt.subplot(2, 2, 4)
plt.plot(x, x**4)      # ma trn  th con 2 dng, 2 ct,  th th t phi di

plt.show()

THÊM CHỮ VÀ CHÚ THÍCH¶

Bn c th thm bt c ch hay ch thch bng cch s dng hm text(), tham s cn truyn vo l to x, y v chui cn thm. Ngoi ra, cc tham s khc cng c th thay i nh v d di y nh: mu sc (color), cn l ngang(horizontalalignment), gc xoay(rotation)...

In [23]:
x = np.linspace(-1.5, 1.5, 30)
px = 0.8
py = px**2

plt.plot(x, x**2, "b-", px, py, "ro")

plt.text(0, 1.5, "Hm s \n$y = x^2$", fontsize=15, color='blue', horizontalalignment="center")
plt.text(px, py, "x = %0.2f\ny = %0.2f"%(px, py), rotation=10, color='gray')

plt.show()

NHÃN VÀ GHI CHÚ¶

Bn c th thm nhn cho mi th v t hm plot() bng cch thm tham s label. Sau , hin th cc nhn ca cc ng bn gi hm legend() vi tham s loc (location) ch v tr xut hin.

plt.show()

def plot_line(axis, slope, intercept, **kargs):
    xmin, xmax = axis.get_xlim()
    plt.plot([xmin, xmax], [xmin*slope+intercept, xmax*slope+intercept], **kargs)

x = np.random.randn(1000)

plt.plot(1, 0, "ro", color='black')

plt.vlines(1, -5, 0, color="green", linewidth=0.75)

plot_line(axis=plt.gca(), slope=0.5, intercept=5, color="blue")

plt.grid(True)
plt.show()

SCATTER PLOTS¶

S dng hm scatter() v th phn tn (scatterplot) ca 10 im d liu c to bi hm random nh sau:

In [38]:
x, y = np.random.rand(2, 10)
plt.scatter(x, y)
plt.show()

Gii thch: hm rand(2, 10), to ra mng 2 chiu hay ni cch khc l 2 mng 1 chiu c s ct l 10; gi tr ca 2 mng ny c gn cho 2 bin x v y, i din to ca 10 cp im.

Bn c th thay i kch thc bng cch s dng tham s s (size) trong hm scatter()

In [51]:
scale = np.random.rand(10)
scale = 500 * scale ** 2
plt.scatter(x, y, s=scale)
plt.show()

Hoc thay i mu sc qua tham s c

In [57]:
for color in ['red', 'green', 'blue']:
    n = 10
    x, y = np.random.rand(2, n)
    scale = 500.0 * np.random.rand(n) ** 2
    plt.scatter(x, y, s=scale, c=color, alpha=0.3)

plt.show()
